Our website is being visited mostly via organic search. This means that most of our consumers or potential consumers are visiting our websites through unpaid search. Users visiting our website from a search engine's organic results have a very specific intent and if we can provide them with a solution or answer to their purpose, they are more likely to convert.

Our home page has a bounce rate of approximately 30%. As an online merchandise store, we want our consumers to view more than one page, so having a high bounce rate is bad. In order to have lower bounce rate, we need to enhance our user experience with our online merchandise store.

Most of our consumers are using desktops to access our online merchandise store. This is most likely going to be the case since we dont have any apps available. Hence, enhancing the user experience via desktop should be important. It is also noteworthy that October and November have low rates of returning visitors (RVR), which means we have more new visitors using our online store during this period.
